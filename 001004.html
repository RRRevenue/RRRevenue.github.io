Social Media Platform Observations

Observations: Facebook

Cover art is clean and simple
Profile photo/icon isn't clear on mobile device.
Posting frequently, however the content being posted is not directly related with restaurant
PTAT (people talking about this) score: 781; With the amounts of likes, the restaurant should be able to engage more of its followers
Average number of likes, comments, shares per post: 36
No posts by fans in the last month
Not asking engaging questions

Recommendations:

Get a version of your profile icon in a higher resolution to avoid blurriness
Post minimum of 1 time per day. Ideally at least 5 times a week. Don't forget weekends, the most engagement happens on the weekends, particularly Saturdays.
Times with highest engagement: 2-5PM
You should ask more questions for more engagement. There are two major advantages to pose questions to your fans. It activates your fans leading to greater visibility for your Page and at the same time providing you with answers to questions that may be crucial for your company's sales and marketing.

Observations: Instagram

Amazing number of likes! Over 27,000.
Multiple posts that do not directly align with the restaurant and its purpose 
Some content posted alienates certain followers 
Not engaging with visitors via Geotags

Recommendations:

Aim for minimum of 1 post per day
Instagram is a great place to exhibit the delicious food the restaurant takes pride in
Open up dialogue for conversation. Encourage followers to engage and/or ask questions o Engage with customers through geotags. Ask about their experience, invite them back. Acknowledge.
